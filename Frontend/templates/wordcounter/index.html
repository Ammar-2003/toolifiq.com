Word Counter FAQs

How does your word counter handle hyphenated words?

We count hyphenated words as single words (e.g., "state-of-the-art" = 1 word). This follows standard publishing and academic conventions.

Are numbers counted as words?

Yes, numbers (including dates and times) are counted as words when separated by spaces (e.g., "2023" or "July 4" each count as one word).

Is my text stored or analyzed beyond word counting?

No. All processing happens in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or analyze your content beyond what's needed to provide the counts you see.

What's the difference between character count with and without spaces?

We currently show total characters (including spaces), which is most useful for social media posts and other space-limited formats.
